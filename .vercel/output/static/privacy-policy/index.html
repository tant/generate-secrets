Our Privacy Story

Hi there,

We’d like to share a simple story about how we protect your privacy. Forget the complicated legal jargon—this is our promise to you, explained in the most human way possible.

Imagine This…

Think of this website as a magic toolbox we send to you. When you open the site, you download this toolbox, and all the secret-generating magic happens right inside your own private space (your web browser).

We, the creators, can never peek inside to see what you’re doing or what secrets you’ve made. Everything stays yours, and yours alone.

Our core promise: your secrets stay yours

Here’s what we absolutely do not do:

  • We never see, process, or touch any secrets you generate.
  • We never store any secrets on our servers. No logs, no caches, nothing at all.
  • We never transmit your secrets anywhere. They never leave your device.
  • We never collect personal data or track how you use this tool.

Put simply: what you create stays with you. Period.

So… do we collect anything?

The honest answer: just a tiny bit, and we want to be fully transparent about it.

We’re curious to know how many people find this tool useful. So, we use Vercel Analytics to count visits. It’s like standing at the door and counting how many people walk into our shop each day.

This tool tells us only very general things, like:

  • Which pages are most popular (e.g., the homepage).
  • Which country visitors are from (e.g., Vietnam).
  • What browser and device people use (e.g., Chrome on desktop).

Most importantly: this tool does not track your identity and cannot see any secrets you generate. It’s just an anonymous counter.

We believe in transparency, so you can read more about Vercel’s privacy policy here.

In short

You create secrets in your own private space (your browser). We simply count how many people visit the site. That’s it.

Thank you for trusting and using our tool. Wishing you safe and secure secret generation!
